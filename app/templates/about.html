What is SplitDif?

SplitDif is a site where users can create and vote on polls.
The more divisive a poll is, the better.
Divisiveness is measured by how close a poll achieves a 50/50 split.
The more divisive polls a user has, the higher their overall ranking.

Users need an account to create and vote on polls.
