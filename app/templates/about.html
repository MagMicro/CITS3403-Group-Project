About SplitDif

What is SplitDif?

SplitDif is a site where users can create and vote on polls. The more divisive a poll is, the better.

Divisiveness is measured by how close a poll achieves a 50/50 split.

Points are awarded corresponding to the amount of votes and the difference between votes.

Users need an account to create and vote on polls.
