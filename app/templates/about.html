About SplitDif

What is SplitDif?

SplitDif is a website dedicated to user created polls:
Users can create polls, with a comparable pair of topics and a prompt.
Other users can then vote on their polls with which option they believe best suits the given prompt.

Important: Users need an account to create and vote on polls.
(However, they can still search polls look at other users posts)

Users can search for polls based on different filters:
  • Most popular (within different timespans)
  • Most recently created
  • Most divisive
  • Particular prompt
  • Particular option/s
  • Based on topics (grouped by tags)
  • Based on a given user
Or a combination of all of the above!

Users can also provide their opinion about a given poll within the comment section
Where they can write new comments for the poll and see what other users had to say and interact with their comments.
(Or debate with them about their decision!)

Nobody likes definitive answers:
The goal is to make the most Divisive polls possible.
That make people really have to think aout their given choice!
Polls that create interaction between users (or arguments...)

Divisiveness is measured by how close a poll achieves a 50/50 split


Compete with other users:
Users are ranked against eachother based on how divisive their posts and how much interaction they are able to get.
The overall ranking for a user is dependant on the amount of points they have accumilated.

Points are awarded by:
  • The amount of votes
  • The difference between votes
